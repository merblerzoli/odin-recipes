Description

A grilled chicken is typically defined as chicken meat that has been cooked over an open flame or heat source, resulting in a charred and smoky flavor. Different methods exist for preparing grilled chicken, such as utilizing high pressure and temperature for improved juiciness, texture, and color

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves.
  • ¼ cup lemon juice, plus wedges for serving.
  • ¼ cup olive oil.
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano or parsley.
  • 1 teaspoon seasoning salt.
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper.
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder.

Steps

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Brush the grill grates with a little oil to prevent sticking.
  4. Place the chicken on the grill and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Flip the chicken over and cook for another 5-7 minutes.
  6. Check that the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Remove from the grill and let the meat rest for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve warm with your favorite side dishes.
